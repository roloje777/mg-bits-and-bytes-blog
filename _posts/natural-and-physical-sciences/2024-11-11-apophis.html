Apophis

Apophis, also known as Apep in Egyptian mythology, was the god of Chaos.
He was the polar opposite of the godly personification of order, Ma'at, and the chief enemy of the king of the gods, the sun-god Ra.
He took the form of a great snake.
This egyptian deity embodied darkness and disorder.

What are asteroid quakes?

The theoretical shaking could happen due to gravitational tidal forces, which are the stretching or pulling effect on an object caused by the uneven gravitational pull from another massive body.
